# Slow-Draining Sink — DIY Fix

**Category:** plumbing
**Urgency level this solves:** LOW
**Time:** 5–10 minutes
**Tools:** boiling water, baking soda, vinegar, optional plunger

## Quick fix (works 80% of the time)

1. Pour 1 cup of **boiling water** down the drain.
2. Add 1/2 cup of **baking soda**. Wait 5 minutes.
3. Pour 1 cup of **white vinegar** + 1 cup of boiling water. Wait 10 minutes.
4. Flush with hot tap water for 30 seconds.

## If it's still slow

Use a plunger:
1. Fill the sink with 2–3 cm of water (covers the plunger cup).
2. Plunge firmly 10 times.
3. Run hot water.

## What this means for Theo Negotiates

Theo Negotiates was designed around *parallel outbound voice calls* via Twilio. Per Wynand's guidance, we are not building real outbound telephony. The auction subsystem is preserved in concept and schema (the business model is still real and valuable), but the **demo theater** changes:

- **Originally:** Three real phones in the room ring, team members answer as Handwerker, AI negotiates live.
- **For the hackathon demo:** Three simulated agent sessions in the dashboard panel, each playing a recorded or web-rendered ElevenLabs voice negotiation, with realistic latency and bid extraction. Stripe operations still fire live on owner consent.

The architecture, schema, and Stripe flows remain identical — only the dialer is mocked. This is documented as such in [`THEO_NEGOTIATES.md`](./THEO_NEGOTIATES.md).
